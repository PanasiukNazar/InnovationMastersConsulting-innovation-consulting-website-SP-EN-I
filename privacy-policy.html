Privacy Policy

Introduction

We are committed to safeguarding the privacy and confidentiality of our clients and website visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information while delivering our innovation consulting services. By using our website and engaging with our offerings, you consent to the practices outlined in this policy.

1. Personal Information

When you interact with our consulting services focused on innovation strategy, product development, creative solutions, and innovation management, we may collect personal details such as your name, contact information, and any other data you choose to share. This information is typically collected through forms, email correspondence, or when you sign up for updates.

2. Usage Data

We collect information about how you engage with our website and services. This includes data such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, the pages you visit, and the date and time of your visit. This helps us enhance our offerings and ensure a seamless user experience.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve user experience. Cookies help us understand your preferences and how you interact with our resources on innovation consulting, including disruptive technologies and product development. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

1. Providing and Improving Services

Your personal information allows us to deliver customized innovation consulting services. Whether focusing on innovation strategy, creative solutions, or managing disruptive technologies, we use this information to tailor our offerings to your unique needs.

2. Communication

We may use your contact details to send updates, newsletters, and information relevant to innovation management and consulting. You can opt out of these communications at any time.

3. Analytics and Research

Usage data and cookies are utilized to analyze trends, improve our website's content, and enhance the effectiveness of our consulting services in areas such as product development and innovation strategy.

We may use your information to fulfill legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. This includes sharing information with legal authorities if required by law.

We may share your information with trusted third-party providers who assist in delivering innovation consulting services. These providers are obligated to maintain confidentiality and use the data solely for the intended purpose.
